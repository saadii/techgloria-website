Privacy Policy

Last updated: 29 May 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Tech Gloria ("we", "us") handles the personal information you provide when you use this website (techgloria.com). We keep things simple: we only collect what you choose to send us, and we use it only to respond to you.

Information we collect

The only personal information we collect is what you submit through our contact form: your name, email address, and the message you write. We do not require you to create an account, and we do not collect this information any other way on the site.

How we use it

We use the details you submit solely to read and respond to your enquiry and to follow up about a potential project. We do not sell, rent, or share your information with third parties for marketing.

Form processing

Our contact form is processed by Web3Forms, a third-party service that receives the submission and forwards it to our inbox. Your message passes through their systems for delivery; please refer to Web3Forms' own privacy policy for details on how they handle data in transit.

Data retention

Contact submissions are retained in our email inbox for as long as needed to handle your enquiry and any resulting work, after which they may be deleted. You can ask us to delete your information at any time.

Your rights

You can request access to, correction of, or deletion of the personal information you have sent us. To make a request, contact us through the form on our website and we will respond within a reasonable time.

Cookies & analytics

This website does not set tracking cookies and does not run third-party analytics. If that changes in the future, we will update this policy first.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.

Contact

Questions about this policy or your data? Reach us via the contact form on our website.
